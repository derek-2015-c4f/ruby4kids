Hello friends! We have a shiny new game for you all to enjoy!
Evolution is a complete remake of one of our old unreleased games. In this fast paced Arcade game, you play as a monster who is trying to evolve! To evolve, you must first kill another player, then find a safe spot to turn into an egg and evolve. The first player who goes through all 6 evolutions wins!
You can play Evolution on Mineplex by heading over to the Arcade section of the Lobby! A special thanks to our developer Mysticate for making this happen! Happy evolving!
We've also added bonus Gems to players who have been online longer! For every hour you are online Mineplex in your current session, you will earn an extra 20% Gems!
